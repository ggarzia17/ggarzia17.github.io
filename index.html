About Me

My name is Gavin. I live in Golden Colorado where I am a 3rd year at Colorado School of Mines. I am currently pursuing a Bachelor's of Science in Computer Science with a focus area in Business. Additionally, I am exploring the intersection of Mathematics and Computer Science by adding a minor in Computational and Applied Math. I am currently involved with several organizations on campus. I am a member of the Evolve Entrepreneurship Club, where I have connected with other entrepreneurially minded students. I am the High School Programming Competition Chair for the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). I was also accepted into the Tau Beta Pi honor society last spring.

When I am not coding, I enjoy getting active by hiking, biking, weightlifting, and playing basketball. I enjoy listening to music and am teaching myself to play the guitar.
